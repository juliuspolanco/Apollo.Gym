OUR HISTORY

The Mighty Apollo

Our gym's legacy traces back to the legendary strongman, Mighty Young Apollo, born as Paul Anderson in 1893. Known for his incredible feats of strength, Apollo gained fame in the early 20th century for performances that included supporting a car and passengers on his chest.

Apollo's strength and showmanship inspired generations, laying the foundation for what would become Apollo Gym. His dedication to physical culture and pushing the limits of human strength continue to influence our gym's philosophy today.

Apollo Gym's Journey

Established in 1990, Apollo Gym carries forward the legacy of strength and dedication embodied by Mighty Young Apollo. For over three decades, we've been at the forefront of fitness innovation, combining time-tested techniques with modern training methods.

Our gym draws upon its special 120-year foundation, spanning three generations of strength enthusiasts. From humble beginnings to becoming a premier 24-hour fitness destination, Apollo Gym has consistently evolved while staying true to its roots in physical culture and strength training.

The Birth of a Legend

Paul Anderson, who would later become known as Mighty Young Apollo, was born in 1893. His incredible journey in the world of strength and physical culture would lay the groundwork for what would eventually become Apollo Gym.

1920

Mighty Apollo gains fame for his incredible feats of strength, including supporting a car and passengers on his chest.

The Rise of Mighty Apollo

By 1920, Mighty Young Apollo had established himself as a legendary strongman, performing incredible feats of strength that would captivate audiences and inspire generations to come. His performances, including supporting a car and passengers on his chest, demonstrated the extraordinary capabilities of human strength.

1990

Apollo Gym's establishment, carrying forward the legacy of strength and dedication embodied by Mighty Young Apollo.

The Birth of Apollo Gym

In 1990, Apollo Gym was established in this iconic building, with its distinctive Art Deco facade becoming a landmark in the area. The gym was founded with a clear vision to carry forward the legacy of strength and dedication embodied by Mighty Young Apollo, while also embracing martial arts and diverse training methods.

The original location featured the prominent "THE MIGHTY APOLLO GYM" signage, paying homage to its namesake while establishing itself as a comprehensive martial arts and strength training center.

2000

Major expansion of facilities and introduction of 24/7 access, making fitness more accessible than ever.

Growth and Innovation

The year 2000 marked a significant milestone with the expansion of facilities and the groundbreaking introduction of 24/7 access. As shown in this historical photo, the gym was equipped with modern training equipment, featuring an extensive free weight area and professional-grade machines. This renovation and expansion made Apollo Gym more accessible to members while maintaining its commitment to excellence in strength training.

2010

Integration of modern training techniques and technology while maintaining traditional strength methods, creating a perfect blend of old and new.

Bridging Tradition and Innovation

2010 marked a significant evolution for Apollo Gym with the introduction of Elite Sports Performance programs and the integration of TeamBuildr technology. This period saw the gym expand its services to include specialized training for sports teams, cycling groups, and performance athletes, while maintaining its core strength training foundation.

The gym began offering comprehensive training programs that combined traditional strength methods with modern sports science, creating a unique training environment that catered to both serious athletes and fitness enthusiasts. The implementation of TeamBuildr technology allowed for better tracking of member progress and more personalized training approaches.

Current Day

Apollo Gym continues to inspire and maintain its legacy as one of Australia's premier strength and fitness facilities.

Legacy Continues

Today, Apollo Gym stands as a testament to its rich history while continuing to evolve and adapt to modern fitness needs. The gym maintains its position as one of Australia's premier strength and fitness facilities, inspiring new generations while honoring its historical roots.
